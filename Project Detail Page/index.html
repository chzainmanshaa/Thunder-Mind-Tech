Tatreez garden

Tatreez Garden is an AI platform for creating patterns inspired by Palestinian embroidery and culturally significant plants.

Project Overview

Tatreez Garden is an AI-driven platform that combines cultural heritage with modern technology, allowing users to create personalized patterns inspired by Tatreez, a traditional Palestinian embroidery. The platform also integrates plants significant to Palestinian ecology and culture, weaving their symbolism into designs. With real-time image generation and an automated workflow, Tatreez Garden celebrates identity, creativity, and the preservation of cultural memory through innovative technology.
